🎯 Design Direction Evaluation Framework

  • Layout Intuitiveness: Which information hierarchy matches your priorities? Does the eye follow the right path (events → claims → consequences)?
  • Authorship Support: Which approach makes players feel like storytellers, not mechanics optimizers? Does claim submission feel central and rewarding?
  • Causality Visibility: How clearly can players trace "I said X → Event includes X"? Are consequences linked back to claims visibly?
  • Replayability Signal: Which direction best shows players "if I claim more about weather, I'll trigger more weather events"? Does strategic learning emerge?
  • Accessibility Readability: Which direction is most readable at 200% zoom? Is text hierarchy clear without color dependence?
  • Emotional Response: Which approach best supports feelings of authorship, agency, and cleverness? Which feels epic rather than mechanical?
